Liberty:
Article 19 <Freedom> 1949: 6 freedoms.
1. To freedom of speach and expression
2. To assemble
 peacefully(without making disturbance or problem to those who are not partcipating with you)
 and without arms(Not absolute)
3. To associtae yourself in any association and union
4. To move freely in India in the public space
5. To reside and settle in any part of India
6. To practice any profession or carry on any occupation
7. (Added 2017) Right to privacy.. whatever you are doing in private, whomever you want 
	to stay Whatever I want to eat is none of Gov. buisness.
